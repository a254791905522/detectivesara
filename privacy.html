Privacy overview

Detective Sara's Hidden Object

Last updated: April 2026

1

Scope

This document summarizes privacy-related information for Detective Sara's Hidden Object, the puzzle game distributed on Apple's App Store. Platform rules and Apple's own policies also apply when you download or update the app.

2

Data collection (per store listing)

The app's App Store privacy label currently states that the developer does not collect data from this app for the categories Apple lists under App Privacy. Practices can change with future updates; check the latest listing on the App Store for the authoritative view.

Note: The game is marked as containing advertising. Ad networks may process data according to their own policies and your device settings—review system ad tracking and privacy options on your iPhone or iPad if you use ad-supported features.
3

Apple & Game Center

Installs, updates, and App Store interactions are handled by Apple. If you use Game Center (for example leaderboards), Apple processes related account and gameplay data under Apple's terms. This page does not replace Apple's documentation.

4

On-device data

Like most mobile games, the app may keep local data on your device—such as level progress or preferences—so play works without constant network use. Unless a future version clearly states otherwise, that information ordinarily stays on your device.

5

Age rating & families

The game is rated 4+. Parents and guardians can use Apple's Screen Time, Family Sharing, and purchase controls to manage access and spending.

6

Changes

This overview may be revised to stay accurate with the app and store listing. The “Last updated” date above will change when the text is edited. For legal or data questions specific to the product, use the support or report options Apple provides on the App Store page for the app.
